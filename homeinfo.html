About Us

Petcare started in 1990 as a one-man mobile veterinary service and has since grown into a one-stop animal wellness centre providing multiple veterinary services catering to the needs and welfare of your pet. We have a team of experienced veterinarians, trained clinic support-staff and friendly office staff who will give you sound advice and always strive to provide optimal care for your pets.

Petcare currently employs 23 dedicated staff: 6 Veterinarians, 3 Clinic Support Staff, 5 Office Admin Staff , 3 Drivers and 6 General workers.

All of Petcare’s veterinarians, care-givers, staff and workers assure more individual interaction and increased attention to the needs of our customers and their pets, as well as for our in-house guest-pets. Each one is committed to your pet’s health and happiness because we understand that your pet means everything to you.
